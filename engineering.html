Engineering


What I do

I'm a UX Engineer at Jump Trading, LLC. I work from the Chicago office. I graduated from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in the winter of 2016 with a Bachelors of Engineering in Computer Science.

Before starting at Jump Trading, I was in Peter Christensen's Big Data for Environmental & Economic Policy group at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications (NCSA)1. In that group, I wrote a thesis on how diverse datasets and novel economic modeling techniques could be used to make time series predictions of rent prices in the US housing market.

Previous academic projects include using convolutional neural nets to classify music genres by examining a song's self-similarity matrix.

Previously I was an intern for Jump Trading, as well as an intern on the OS X Frameworks team, AppKit, with Apple, in Cupertino, California. It was awesome, and I highly recommend the Apple intern experience.

I have experience with a lot of platforms, languages and domains; I've working on everything from graphics2 to interpreters3, VR4 to mobile (as a freelance developer)5, data science to CS theory5. Learning is a favorite pastime of mine.
